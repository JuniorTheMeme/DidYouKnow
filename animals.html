Fun fact: there are over 10 million animals in the world, and we haven't even discovered half of them

1. The regal horned lizard has a gross way of repelling attackers. They squirt blood out of their eyes!

2. Scientists estimate that dogs were domesticated (made human-compatible) 15,000 years ago.

3. Polar bears are not really white. Their fur is actually transparent.

4. An ostrich’s eyes are bigger than its brain.

5. Bats are the only mammals that can fly.

6. A penny can weigh up to four grams, but some hummingbirds weigh only two grams!

7. Gorillas burp when they’re happy!

8. Cheetahs only need to drink once every three to four days.

9. Much like tree rings, whale earwax accumulates in layers, adding a layer about every six months.
By slicing up the wax, a scientist can estimate a whale’s age.

10. Starfish have no brain and no blood. And they aren’t even fish!

11. Baby elephants are able to stand within 20 minutes of being born.

12. Reindeer grow new antlers every year.

13. Sloths are so slow that in their native climate, algae actually grows on their fur.

14. Some land snails can sleep for up to three years in hibernation or aestivation.

15. A grizzly bear’s bite is so strong that it can crush a bowling ball.

16. Rats laugh when being tickled.

17. There are no male or female earthworms.

18. A jaguar’s night vision is six times better than a human’s.

19. Humans share 98.8% of chimpanzee DNA.

20. A rhinoceros horn is made from compacted hair.
